Directions

Route 1

Unpin from home
  1. From JR Hakata, change trains to Subway (Kuko Line) [200JPY]
  2. Get off at the Nakasu Kawabata station [K09] [about 3min]
  3. Walk out of Exit No.5. (Exit for Kawabata) exterior view of Fukuoka Hana Hostel
  4. On the way upstairs. The stair case splits into two. You MUST take the right staircase, and then you will come out in the shopping arcade.
  5. Go left and walk along on the shopping arcade for 400m. And you will find us, closer to the end of the shopping arcade, on your right side. The shopping street will cross over two back streets. Stay under the roof of the shopping arcade until you reach us!

Hotel Policies

Arrival

Unpin from home

If you arrive before check-in time, you can leave your luggage in the storage room during the reception hours.

During the off-hours, the frontdoor in locked for security. So please arrive during the reception hours.

Once you check-in, you can go out and come back any time with the room key and the entrance key.

Introduction

Right in the middle of Hakata! 2 min walk to the yatai area, 5 min walk to a subway sta., 12min walk to Tenjin area, 15 min walk to JR Hakata. Our bicycle rental is the perfect way to get around and explore the city at your pace.

Fukuoka Hana Hostel is opened in March, 2013 with the aim of making your stay as cozy as possible. The 4th hostel of the hostel chain 'Hana Hostel', which has rich experiences and knowledge to welcome our guests from all over the world.

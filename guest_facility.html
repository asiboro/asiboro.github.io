Time

Check-in time 15:00 to 22:00.

Check-out time 8:00 to 11:00.

Reception hours : 8:00-14:00 & 15:00-22:00

We have no curfew and lockout.

Arrival

If you arrive before check-in time, you can leave your luggage in the storage room during the reception hours.

During the off-hours, the frontdoor in locked for security. So please arrive during the reception hours.

Once you check-in, you can go out and come back any time with the room key and the entrance key.

Payment

Please make your payment for your whole nights to stay on your arrival.

Credit Cards accepted (VISA or MASTER)

Others

Smoking is not allowed inside of the building.

Please help management to save electricity and water.

We will keep an unclaimed lost article for 3 month.

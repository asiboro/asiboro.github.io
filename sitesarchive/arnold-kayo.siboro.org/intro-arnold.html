Dear friends,

I believe you came over this page because you knew me, so this page is not really an introductory on me. Rather, in this page I would like to tell you what happened to me since we parted (perhaps more than 10 years ago)

Upon graduating from SMA Cendana Rumbai in 1991, I moved to Bandung and entered Physics Department of Institute Teknologi Bandung. In 1993 I decided to accept the scholarship offered by Japanese Government and go to Tokyo, Japan. In this country I studied for my associate, bachelor and master degree in several different but related fields.

I decided to have enough with schooling and started working for Fujitsu in Fukuoka a month after I got my master degree in Computer Science in March 2001.

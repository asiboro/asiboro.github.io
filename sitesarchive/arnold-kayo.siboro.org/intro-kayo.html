Dear friends,

I think many of those of you who came visiting this site did not know me. Here on these pages I will do my best to introduce myself.

I grew up in rural area of Oita Prefecture in Japan named Tsukumi. Upon graduating from senior high school I went outside the city I grew up to Onomichi which is located in Hiroshima Prefecture. After finishing my tertiary study there I took some courses back in Oita after which I managed to pass national test in Tokyo to become civil servant. I then was positioned at Kyushu Institute of Technology, a national university in Iizuka, Fukuoka Prefecture. I am still working at this university now.
